How to move pieces

To move a piece you just hold the mouse over it and hold it, then lose the grip where you want it placed In order to move a piece, two conditions need to be filled. the first one being that it have to your turn, second the move must be allowed.

And if you tutch a piece it wont move unless you dropp it over a square that is highlighted.
